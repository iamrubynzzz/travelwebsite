Popular Destinations

Pokhara

Pokhara, known as the "Tourist Capital of Nepal," is the second-largest city in Nepal, following Kathmandu. Situated at an altitude of over 900 meters, it stands as one of the highest cities in the region, serving as a starting point for several famous treks around the world.

Annapurna Base Camp (ABC)

The ABC Trek is widely recognized as one of Nepal's most renowned trekking routes. It offers a relatively easier and shorter journey compared to other high-altitude treks in the country. This trek presents a mix of varied landscapes, cultural experiences, and encounters with wildlife. Moreover, it provides trekkers with breathtaking and up-close views of iconic mountains such as Annapurna, Machhapuchhre, Hiunchuli, and Dhaulagiri.

Pahupatinath Temple

The Pashupatinath Temple, established around the beginning of the 17th century, is the oldest temple in Kathmandu and holds huge religious significance in Nepal. Located along the Bagmati River in western Kathmandu, this temple stands as a prime example of beautiful pagoda-style architecture, decorated with copper and gold decorations.

Lumbini

Lumbini, situated in the Terai plains of Nepal, holds great significance as the birthplace of Lord Buddha. It is a highly respected pilgrimage destination for Buddhists worldwide. Lumbini attracts over 400,000 visitors annually, including both Buddhists and non-Buddhists. Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site (Culture), Lumbini carries immense archaeological and religious importance.

Upper Mustang

The isolated and lesser-known Upper Mustang has preserved its cultures and traditions for a long time. It has significant cultural and historical importance. The kingdom of Lo has Buddhist devotees. There is no better place than Upper Mustang if you wish to learn about unspoiled Tibetan and Buddhist traditions and cultures.

Shivapuri

Shivapuri offers great day hike and a nice way to shake off the jet lag before heading to base camp or the higher mountains. The most popular treks near to Kathmandu for bird watcher group travelers. The good thing about hiking in the Shivapuri area is that there are numerous options – whether to hike for just 2, 3 hours or for an entire day – there are choices.

Ghandruk

Ghandruk, the famous Gurung village at 2,010m (6,600ft), is located northwest of the lakeside city of Pokhara. It is accessible in one day, with a four to five-hour walk after a short ride from the city. The major attraction of Ghandruk is the unique culture of the Gurungs living in the area. It entertains the tourists with the Gurung dances and allows the tourists with the opportunity to wear traditional Gurung dresses and ornaments.

Badimalika

Badimalika trek is one of the best treks in west Nepal providing an experience of high passes, ethnic villages, astonishing natural beauty, spiritual experience, the lifestyle of locals, green hills, white Himalayas, remote natural campsite, wilderness trail, and landscape of the pristine part of Nepal. Badimalika Temple, located in the Bajura district of Seti Zone, is dedicated to Bhagwati.
