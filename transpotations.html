Mode of Transportation

Buses

Buses are widely used for both short and long-distance travel within Nepal. They are a cost-effective mode of transportation and connect major cities, towns, and rural areas. Local buses, tourist buses, and deluxe buses are available depending on the route and comfort level.

Domestic Flights

Domestic flights are a popular mode of transportation for long-distance travel within Nepal, especially to remote areas or between major cities like Kathmandu, Pokhara, Bharatpur, Biratnagar, and Nepalgunj.

Taxis

Taxis are commonly used for transportation within cities and towns. They can be easily hailed on the streets or hired from taxi stands. In major cities like Kathmandu and Pokhara, you will find both regular taxis and app-based ride-hailing services.

Motorbikes or Scooters

Renting motorbikes or scooters is a popular choice, especially for adventure enthusiasts and backpackers. It offers flexibility and freedom to explore different parts of Nepal at your own pace. However, make sure to have a valid license and wear appropriate safety gear.
